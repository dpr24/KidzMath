KidzMath Privacy Policy

We know how important your privacy is. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information through our app, KidzMath.

What information do we collect?

We don't collect any personal information about you or your child. This means we don't collect:

  • Location information

We don't use any information for any purpose.

No. We don't share any information with third parties, including advertising companies or data analytics providers.

Children's privacy:

We take children's privacy very seriously. Our app is designed to be safe for children of all ages. We comply with all applicable children's privacy laws, including the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Changes to this privacy policy:
